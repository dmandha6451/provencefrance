P R O V E N C E,   F R A N C E

Provence, France has and will always be one of my favorite places to visit. Located a bit away from the major, and typical tourist attractions such as the Eiffel Tower, Provence provides a calm and serene beauty, perfect for one to rest and enjoy themselves. From the gorgeous blue waters to bright lavender fields, Provence has scenery like no other, and the food never disappoints.
